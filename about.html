Travel Assistance

I'll tell you my story. I'm an immigrant. We came like any other traveler in search of achieving our dream of freedom and quality of life. When I arrived in this country, I started working in a company because of the need to earn an income and because of all kinds of problems in my hometown. As the place, I was mistreated at work and persecuted by an influential person, supposedly to help me. As a result of that situation, I got sick. I was on bed rest for almost two months. I had flu symptoms, cough, nasal congestion, and chest pains that did not improve. The pain continued without any improvement. Even when I breathed, my chest hurt. Thank God I was able to recover relatively well, because now when I cough or sneeze, I have chest pains.I was also scammed with a Honda vehicle, I think by the same people who persecuted me, chased me, causing me to lose money and endangering my personal safety, since the vehicle had serious mechanical faults. I was advised by a people mechanic who works for the Honda company, but it was all a fraud. Thank you to God, My true friends were and are protecting care me, I feel very grateful for that.

My idea with this project is to convey the need to be insured when traveling to any country other than where you live, so that you can safeguard first of all the health of yourself and your family, secondly your financial assets, and finally the peace of mind of knowing that you have financial support at the time that any risk may materialize, according to statistical science and probabilities.
